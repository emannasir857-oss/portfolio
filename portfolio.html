My Story

I live at the intersection of logic and aesthetics — one half of me is a Computer Science student who enjoys the discipline of solving a problem in clean, working code, and the other half is a designer who can't leave that solution alone until it looks and feels right too.

That mix is what pulls me toward UI/UX and graphic design: I like building things people actually want to use, not just things that technically work. Add social media marketing into the picture, and it's really one skill — understanding what draws people in, whether that's a well-structured function or a well-placed pixel.

I believe in practical execution over grades. A transcript can't build, design, or ship — working projects can. That's the standard I hold myself to.
